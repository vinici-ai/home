The Problem: A Crisis of Clarity

Politicians often say one thing and do another. But in today’s flood of 24/7 news, social media, and complex speeches, seeing the mismatch is nearly impossible.

The truth gets buried in an overwhelming volume of data, leaving manipulation in the gray space between words and actions. Accountability is lost.

This isn't just a political issue; it's a barrier to progress and trust, felt in democracies and transitional governments worldwide.

Our Solution: Expertise-Led, AI-Powered

Vinici changes this.

By uniting the deep knowledge of political experts with the scale of artificial intelligence, our platform gives you a clear view of the political landscape.

We empower citizens, journalists, and researchers to track promises, uncover genuine alignment, and separate rhetoric from reality.

  • ✓ Detect manipulative rhetoric
  • ✓ Track promises vs. reality
  • ✓ Flag contradictions in real-time
  • ✓ Translate complex data into clear insights

Proof in Practice: The Georgia 2024 Pilot

We piloted Vinici in Georgia, where leaders claimed pro-EU values while passing anti-democratic laws. Our system provided citizens with real-time clarity on this contradiction.

The AI flagged manipulative language, tracked broken promises, and highlighted rhetorical shifts as they happened.

The outcome? In just 10 days, we drove over 20,000 website visits and 2,000+ chatbot engagements, helping citizens make informed choices.

Our Hybrid Intelligence Methodology

1

The Expert Framework

Our political scientists build the analytical framework (the ViniciGraph), defining what to look for—from policy topics to specific propaganda techniques.

2

AI at Scale

Our specialized AI agents apply this framework to millions of data points, classifying statements and tracking narratives with superhuman speed.

3

Human-in-the-Loop Validation

Our experts continuously audit, correct, and refine the AI's work, ensuring our analysis is nuanced, accurate, and accountable.
