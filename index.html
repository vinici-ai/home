Profiling Politicians: Beyond the Rhetoric

Know them by what they say, hold them by what they do.

Try Vinici Learn More

The Problem

Political communication often relies on propaganda and manipulative rhetoric to influence public opinion. Without tools to detect manipulation in real time, it goes unnoticed and accountability comes too late.

Georgia, our home country, is a real example. As an EU candidate, we had high hopes for EU Integration and democratic progress. But today, actions of the pro-Russian ruling party are pulling us away from our European path.

This isn't just Georgia's story. Around the world, politicians say one thing and do another, leaving citizens frustrated and disillusioned with democratic processes.

Our Solution

Vinici profiles politicians based on what they actually say and what they do. Our analysis cuts through the persuasive PR talk and shows what is really behind politicians' messages.

We flag manipulative language, track broken promises, and reveal contradictions in political communication, putting this powerful tool directly in citizens' hands.

Our AI chatbot is capable of not only analyzing rhetoric but also building knowledge graphs that are easy to understand for everyone, regardless of their political expertise.

Key Features

Speech Pattern Analysis

Our AI detects manipulative rhetoric techniques in real-time, highlighting problematic language patterns used to mislead the public.

Promise Tracker

We systematically monitor commitments made by politicians and compare them to their actual actions, creating a transparent accountability record.

Inconsistency Detection

Our system flags contradictions in messaging over time, revealing when politicians change their stance without acknowledging the shift.

Citizen-Friendly Interface

Complex political analysis is presented in an accessible way, making it easy for every citizen to understand the true nature of political communication.

About Vinici

Vinici was born from a passion for democracy and transparency in political communication. Our mission is to empower citizens with the tools they need to see beyond rhetoric and hold their elected officials accountable.

We believe that a well-informed citizenry is essential for a functioning democracy, and that technology can be a powerful ally in the fight against political manipulation and propaganda.

Our team consists of experts in AI, political science, linguistics, and civic technology, all united by a common goal: to strengthen democratic institutions through transparency and accountability.
