Our Hybrid Intelligence Methodology

We combine the nuanced knowledge of human experts with the scale of specialized AI. This is how.

1

The Expert Framework

Our political scientists build the analytical framework (the ViniciGraph), defining what to look for—from policy topics to specific propaganda techniques like 'Whataboutism' and 'Fear Appeals'.

2

AI at Scale

Our specialized AI agents apply this framework to millions of data points, classifying statements and tracking narratives with superhuman speed.

3

Human-in-the-Loop Validation

Our experts continuously audit, correct, and refine the AI's work, ensuring our analysis is nuanced, accurate, and accountable.

Our Methodology in Action: The Georgia 2024 Pilot

We piloted Vinici in Georgia, where leaders claimed pro-EU values while passing anti-democratic laws. Our system provided citizens with real-time clarity on this contradiction.

The AI flagged manipulative language, tracked broken promises, and highlighted the direct contradiction between words and actions.

The outcome? In just 10 days, we drove over 20,000 website visits and 2,000+ chatbot engagements, empowering citizens to see beyond the rhetoric.
