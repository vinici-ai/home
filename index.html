The Problem

Politicians say one thing and do another. Rhetoric is the mask. Actions tell the truth.

But until now, there’s been no way for citizens to see the mismatch in real time.

This isn't just a local issue. It's global. From Western democracies to transitional governments, manipulation lives in the gray space between language and behavior.

Our Solution

Vinici shows you the truth politicians hope you miss.

Our AI listens to what they say, tracks what they do, and reveals where they don’t match.

• Detects manipulative rhetoric

• Tracks unkept promises

• Flags contradictions

• Translates results into plain language
